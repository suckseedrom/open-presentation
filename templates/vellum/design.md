# Vellum — Design System

## Visual thesis

Keep the same cinematic shell, but slow the emotional temperature. This template should feel quiet, assured, and designed for serious thinking.

Keep the scenes very sparse: text-light, almost textless where possible, with motion carrying the mood.

## Emphasis

- deep navy or near-black canvases
- warm serif contrast with understated accent color
- product mockups that feel elegant and contemplative, not loud
- motion should remain tactile but lower-amplitude
- if copy starts to feel explanatory, split the beat into another scene
