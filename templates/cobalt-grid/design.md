# Cobalt Grid — Design System

## Visual thesis

This template applies a sharper, design-research lens to the shared video-presentation system.

Keep the scenes label-light and text-light so the grid and motion stay crisp.

## Emphasis

- strong grid structure and cobalt-led accents
- highly legible analytical product states
- premium data proof scenes and disciplined whitespace
- motion should be restrained, exact, and highly intentional
- copy should be terse and diagram-friendly, not paragraph-like
