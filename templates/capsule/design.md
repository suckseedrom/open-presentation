# Capsule — Design System

## Visual thesis

Preserve the same ad pacing, but soften the interface into modular rounded product surfaces with friendly clarity.

Keep the scenes concise and text-light; this template should feel friendly, not verbose.

## Emphasis

- pill-shaped cards and rounded containers
- warmer, brighter accents
- clean multi-card layouts and comparison modules
- motion should feel refined and playful, never childish
- copy should be short enough to read at a glance while motion keeps moving

## Background and mobile rules

- Every scene must have a static, full-viewport background layer rendered outside the scaled stage.
- Backgrounds must not be animated (no drifting gradients, no scale transforms).
- After composing at 16:9, recheck every scene at 9:16.
- On mobile: stack horizontal layouts, cap headlines at 3rem, keep 40px safe-zone padding, and remove any overflow.
